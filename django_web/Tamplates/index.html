Something about Konkan..!

Greenery...!

Konkan's greenery, coconut trees, beautiful virgin beaches, waterfalls, mountains and lush green valleys will definitely provide a rich and pleasant experience for the traveler.

Fort's...!

Sindhudurg Fort is a historical fort that occupies an island in the Arabian Sea. The fort was built by chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The fortress lies on the shore of Malvan town of Sindhudurg District in the Konkan region of Maharashtra.

Great Sea...!

Konkan, a beautiful place which is as good as a paradise, a coastline at the western part of India. The sandy golden beaches with crystal clear ocean are the main attractions for all the travelers across the world to visit Konkan at least once in a lifetime.

Konkan Culture and People...!

This region is a naturally gifted location of the state. The individuals are literate and usually well off. They are a normally peace loving people who are practical and friendly to Tourists. They depend mainly upon fishing and farming for their income and nowadays some are making their living with the assistance of growing Tourism in the area. The areas of Devgad, Dapoli and Ratnagiri are well-known for its Alphonso mangoes and also a lot of fish are exported from Ratnagiri port.

Maharashtra’s 720-km. shoreline and the adjoining area or ‘Konkan’ as it is called, extends from Dahanu in the north up to Goa in south. Konkan is well known for its natural appeal and is quick emerging as a preferred traveler location for travelers. Konkan’s greenery, coconut trees, stunning virgin beaches, waterfalls, mountains and rich green valley’s will definitely supply an abundant and pleasant experience for the tourist.
